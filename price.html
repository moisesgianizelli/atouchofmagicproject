Classes

To help budding bakers that can’t wait for in person classes, I have made an Online Kids Baking Camp, which gives immediate access to 5 x 1 hour baking classes so that kids can take the classes at home, with a little assistance from a parent if needed. The beauty of the precorded sessions is that they can watch them in any order, pause if needed, watch them over and over again on whatever day they choose. Let them take over the kitchen, learn a life skill, treat the whole family & have fun while doing so!
